# Account sitting

Going away? A trusted friend can keep your account running while you're gone — **account sitting**. Find it
under **Sitting** in the top bar.

## Authorising a sitter

On the Sitting page, add a player by username under **Your sitters**. They can then operate your account —
build, train, send troops, trade, message — but they **cannot**:

- change your settings or your profile,
- manage your sitters, or
- hand your account to someone else.

You can **revoke** a sitter at any time; if they're operating your account at that moment, they're dropped
back to their own immediately.

## Sitting for someone

If another player has authorised you, they appear under **Accounts you sit for**. Click **Sit** to take
over — a banner across the top reminds you whose account you're operating, with a **Stop sitting** button to
return to your own. While sitting, everything you do happens as the owner.

## The audit log

Everything a sitter does on your account is recorded under **Recent sitter activity** on your Sitting page —
who did what, so trust is verifiable.

## Fair play

Sitting can't be used to dodge a ban: a suspended or banned player can't sit, and a suspended or banned
account can't be operated by a sitter. The usual rules (and the end-of-round freeze) apply throughout.
