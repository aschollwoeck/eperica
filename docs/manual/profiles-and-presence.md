# Your profile & who's online

Eperica gives you a little identity and shows you who's around.

## Your bio

Open **Profile** from the top bar. There you can write a short **bio** — plain text, up to 500 characters —
that appears on your public statistics page for other players to read. Edit it any time and click **Save**.
Only you can change your own bio.

## Presence — online or last seen

Next to a player's name you'll see a small presence marker:

- **● online** — they were active just now (within the last few minutes).
- **○ last seen …** — how long ago they were last active (minutes, hours, or days).

You'll find it in the places where you already look at other players:

- their **public stats page**,
- the **leaderboards** (next to each player's name),
- your **conversations** list and the header of a direct-message thread,
- and on **map** village markers (in the tile's label).

Presence is just a hint about activity — it updates as you move around the game, and it never reveals
anything private about an account (your troops, resources, and the like always stay hidden). Group channels
like Global and Alliance don't show a single presence, since many players share them.
