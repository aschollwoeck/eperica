# Notifications & alerts

Eperica keeps a **bell** in the top bar so you never miss something important — even while you're off
looking at the map or your queues.

## What you're alerted about

- **Incoming attack** — the moment an enemy launches an attack or raid at one of your villages, you're
  warned (with the target and roughly when it arrives), so you have time to react.
- **Battle report** — when a battle you were part of resolves (as the attacker, the defender, or a
  reinforcer), you're told the report is ready.
- **New message** — when another player sends you a direct message.

## The bell

The 🔔 in the top bar shows your unread count. It updates on its own — both by checking periodically and,
when you have the game open, the instant something happens. Click it to open your **notifications** feed,
newest first. Each entry links straight to what it's about: the report, the conversation, or the spot on
the map. Opening the feed clears the bell.

## Private to you

Your notifications are yours alone — no one else can see or clear them, and the live bell only ever
delivers your own. A missed live update is never lost: every notification is saved, so it's still waiting
in your feed the next time you look.
