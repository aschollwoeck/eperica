# Settings & preferences

The **Settings** page (in the top bar) is where you tailor Eperica to your taste.

## Notification preferences

Right now Settings lets you choose which **notifications** you want. Each kind has a checkbox:

- **Incoming attack** — warnings when an enemy targets one of your villages.
- **Battle report** — when a battle you were in resolves.
- **New message** — when someone sends you a direct message.

All are on by default. Untick a kind and click **Save** to stop being notified about it — you won't get a
bell, a feed entry, or a live alert for that kind from then on (anything you were already sent stays in your
feed). Tick it again to start receiving them once more.

Your settings are private and only you can change them.
