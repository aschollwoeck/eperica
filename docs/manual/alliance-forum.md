# The alliance forum

Your alliance has its own **forum** — a private place to plan and talk that sticks around (unlike live
chat). Find it from the alliance page: **Your role: … · Forum**.

## Threads & replies

The forum is a list of **threads**, newest activity first. Open one to read its posts oldest-first, and add
a **reply** at the bottom. To start a new thread, fill in a title and your first post at the bottom of the
forum page. Every member of your alliance can read threads, start them, and reply.

## Announcements

Members with the **Announce** right (the founder, or a leader granted it) can mark a new thread as an
**announcement**. Announcements are a one-way broadcast: they show with an "Announcement" badge and are
**locked** — everyone reads them, but no one replies. Use them for rules, calls to arms, and notices.

## Private to your alliance

The forum belongs to your alliance alone. Only current members can read or post; players in other alliances
(or none) can't see it, and if you leave the alliance you lose access. Everything is enforced by the
server.
