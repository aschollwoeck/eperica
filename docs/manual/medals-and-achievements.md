# Medals & achievements — prestige

Beyond the live leaderboards, the world records lasting prestige: **medals** for topping a weekly
category, and **achievements** for reaching milestones.

## Weekly medals

Once a week (real time — every 7 days, regardless of world speed), the server runs a **settlement**. It
takes a snapshot of everyone's population and awards **medals** to the top performers of that week in
each category:

- **Top attacker / defender / raider** — most attack points, defence points, and resources looted during
  the week.
- **Top climber** — most population **gained** over the week (the snapshot makes this possible).
- **Top alliances** — by aggregate member population and by aggregate attack/defence points.

The top few in each category (configurable) earn a medal recording the category, rank, and week. Medals
are **permanent** — they stay on your profile forever and accumulate week after week. Your medals appear
on your statistics page; alliance medals appear on the alliance's page.

## Achievements

**Achievements** are one-time badges for crossing a milestone. The starter set:

- **Found a second village**
- **Win 100 defensive battles**
- **Occupy your first oasis**
- **Reach 1000 population**
- **Research every unit of your tribe**

Each is granted automatically the first time you meet it — once only — and some carry a small **reward**
(culture points, or resources delivered to your capital). Your earned achievements show on your
statistics page.

## Climbers & history (new boards)

With weekly snapshots in place, the leaderboard gains a **Top climbers** category (who grew the most last
week), and every statistics page shows your **population over time**.
