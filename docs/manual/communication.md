# Communication — messages & chat

Eperica has one place to talk to other players: **Messages**. It works like a modern chat app — a list of
conversations, each a running thread that updates live.

## Conversations

Open **Messages** from the top bar. You'll see your conversations, newest first: your **direct-message**
threads with other players, plus the **group channels** you can see — a **Global** channel everyone shares,
and your **Alliance** channel (if you're in one). Each row shows the latest line and an **unread** count;
the nav badge shows your total unread.

## Direct messages

To message someone, open their profile (from the leaderboard or a stats page) and click **Message** — that
opens your conversation with them. Type in the box and send. Both of you see the same running thread; new
lines appear live. Opening a conversation marks it read.

## Channels

- **Global** — open to every player.
- **Alliance** — only members of your alliance can read or post it.

Posting to a channel you're not allowed in is refused by the server.

## Live updates

Messages appear in an open conversation within about a second, without reloading. (Under the hood they're
delivered over a live stream; if your connection drops, the full history is still there when you reload —
nothing is lost.)

## Rules

Everything goes through the server: you can only read your own conversations and the channels you're allowed
in. Sending is **rate-limited** (so no one can flood a channel), and an account that's **suspended or
banned** for a fair-play violation can't send (though it can still read). See something abusive? Report the
player from their profile — moderators review reports and can sanction.
