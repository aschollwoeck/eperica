# Getting started

> This page helps you create an account, log in, and view your first village.

## Create your account

To play Eperica you need an account.

1. Open the site and choose **Create account**.
2. Enter a **username**, your **email**, and a **password** (at least 8 characters).
3. Submit the form.

Your account is created and, in most worlds, you are taken straight to your new village. (If the world
requires email confirmation, you'll be asked to confirm your email before your first login.)

## Your starting village

Every new player begins with one **village**. It already has:

- **18 resource fields** that will produce **wood**, **clay**, **iron**, and **crop**, and
- a **Main Building** and a **Rally Point** to build from.

Your village sits at a coordinate on the shared world map — that's your home base.

## Log in and out

1. Choose **Log in**.
2. Enter your **username** and **password**.

You'll arrive at your village. Use **Log out** when you're done; your progress is saved on the server,
so everything is exactly as you left it next time you log in.

## Tips

- Keep your password safe — it's the only way into your account.
- If login says *"invalid username or password"*, double-check both; the message is intentionally vague
  for security.
