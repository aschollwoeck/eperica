# Building & upgrading

> This page explains how to grow your village by upgrading fields and constructing buildings.

## The build queue

Your village builds **one thing at a time**. When you order an upgrade, it costs resources
immediately and then takes time to finish — you'll see what's under construction with a live countdown
on your village page. When it completes, the new level applies automatically (even if you're offline).

## Upgrading resource fields

Each resource field can be upgraded to produce more per hour. On your village page, each field shows
its level and the cost of the next level; if you can afford it and nothing else is building, press
**Upgrade**.

## Buildings

- **Main Building** — upgrade it to make *all* your construction faster.
- **Warehouse** — raises how much wood, clay, and iron you can store.
- **Granary** — raises how much crop you can store.
- **Rally Point** — used later for troops.

Some buildings have **prerequisites** (for example, a Warehouse needs a Main Building first). If a
build isn't available yet, the page will say so.

## Tips

- Build a **Warehouse** and **Granary** early so production isn't wasted once your stores fill up.
- Upgrade your **Main Building** to speed up everything that follows.
